taggy

helps you tag your content

Tagging content in web-based editorial systems, i.e. assigning content to a selection of topics

is mostly still done manually and therefore tedious and error-prone especially when it comes to large amounts of data.

taggy is here to give you an open-source frontend module to help you with your tagging needs.

Documentation

It's yours

Proprietary systems are often expensive and questionable in terms of data protection. taggy is open and your data stays with you.

Ready to go

Get going fast. Define some triggers words and start to tag your content.

Easy to connect

Our focus is on easy integration. Currently targeted: Vanilla JS and Angular. More to come!
